## Privacy Policy for Lara

Last updated: 22.11.2025

This privacy policy explains how Lara (“the App”) collects, uses, and protects your information. We are committed to respecting your privacy and complying with Google Play policies.

### Information We Collect

1. Diagnostics and Performance Data (Enabled by Default)

The App collects diagnostic information to help us understand app performance and improve stability. This may include:

- Crash reports
- Performance metrics
- Feature usage information
- Device type and OS version
- App installation ID (a random, non-personal identifier)

No names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other personally identifiable information are collected.

2. Installation ID

The App generates an installation-specific ID used to associate diagnostics data with a specific app installation. This ID cannot identify you personally.

3. Location Data

The App uses your device’s GPS locally to perform offline navigation based on GPX routes.
No location data is uploaded or stored on our servers.

### Why We Collect This Data

We use diagnostics data to:

- Improve app reliability
- Understand crashes and bugs
- Optimize performance
- Prioritize feature development

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics unrelated to diagnostics, profiling, or third-party tracking.

### Your Choices

You can disable the upload of diagnostics data at any time:

In the App:
Settings → Developer → Disable diagnostics upload

When disabled, no diagnostics data will be sent to our servers.

### Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties.
Diagnostics data is stored securely on our servers and used only for troubleshooting and improving the App.

### Data Security

We take appropriate technical measures to protect the data we collect, including secure communication and controlled server access.

### Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

### Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
